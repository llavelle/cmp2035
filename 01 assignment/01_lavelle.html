Quinoa Crust Pizza

This plant-based pizza recipe is equal parts nutritious and delicious! It is vegan, flourless, gluten-free, and high in protein and fiber.

Quinoa Crust

  • 3/4 cup uncooked quinoa
  • 1/4 cup water
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt

Basil-Hemp Seed Pesto

  • Basil-Hemp Seed Pesto
  • 2 tablespoons hemp seeds
  • 3 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1/2 teaspoon sea salt

Toppings

  • 1/4 cup cherry tomatoes, quartered
  • 1/4 cup kalamata olives, halved
  • 2-3 garlic cloves, minced
  • 6 cremini mushrooms, sliced
  • 1/2 yellow onion, sliced

Instructions

  1. Soak quinoa in water for 8 hours, and then drain and rinse.
  2. Preheat oven to 425 degrees.
  3. Place quinoa in food processor along with remaining crust ingredients.
  4. Pulse for two minutes until a paste-like substance forms. (It will still be a little grainy.)
  5. Line pie tin with parchment paper, and pour the quinoa mixture into the tin.
  6. Smooth with a spatula, and bake in the oven for 15 minutes.
  7. In the meantime, you can prep the toppings. Saute the mushrooms in 1 tablespoon olive oil and half of the minced garlic. Caramelize the onions in a pan by constantly stirring them over low heat with 1 tablespoon olive oil. When they begin to become translucent, add the remaining minced garlic, and cook for another couple of minutes.
  8. Take the crust out of the oven, and carefully remove both the crust and the parchment paper from the tin.
  9. Flip the crust, replace it back into the tin, and bake for another 10 minutes.
  10. While the crust is baking, make the pesto by putting all the ingredients in a food processor and pulsing until combined. How the Pesto should look
  11. Remove the crust from the oven. Looks like this Leave it in the tin, and cover with pesto and toppings.
  12. Replace in the oven, and cook for another 10 minutes.
  13. Remove the pizza from the oven one final time. Allow to cool slightly before removing it from the tin, slicing, and serving. Hopefully the final product will look like this
